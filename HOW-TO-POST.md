# How to post on the Efficiency Media blog

Hi Leah — publishing a post takes about two minutes:

1. Go to **https://efficiency-media.sanity.studio** and log in (use the email Josh invited).
2. Click **Posts → "Create new Post"**, then add a **Title**, a one-line **Excerpt**, a **Cover image** (with a short description in the *Alt text* box), and write your article in the **Body**.
3. Click the **Publish** button (bottom-right).
4. That's it — your post appears at **efficiencymedia.com/blog** within a few seconds. No need to email anyone.
